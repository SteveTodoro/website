POWER-UPS 
===========

67 Power: 
All answers become six seven.

Sniper Shot :
Aim and shoot the correct answer automatically (once per round).


Melon Splash: 
Eliminate 2 wrong answer choices at once (up to 3x per round).

Sushi Time: 
Freeze the timer for 2 seconds (up to 3x per round). 

Play the game to unlock more power ups!
