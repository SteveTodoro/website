ABOUT

Pixel Math is the retro arcade math game for kids aged 5–12. Fast rounds + unlockable power-ups turn boring math drills into meme-powered madness. Kids will level up their math skills rapidly and naturally through quick fire quiz rounds that put the fun back into learning.

THREE MODES. ENDLESS EXCITEMENT.

Practice Mode — No time limit, no pressure!

Game Mode — Race the clock! Earn streaks, charge your power gauge, unleash epic combos.

Challenge Mode — 9 increasingly tough levels that test your limits.

COLLECT 79 UNIQUE PIXEL ART STICKERS

Unlock a multitude of hand-crafted pixel art characters bursting with charm. From viral internet memes, to video game legends and pop stars. Build the ultimate sticker album!

UNLOCK POWER-UPS THAT FLIP THE SCRIPT

Freeze the timer, simplify the question, or turn every answer into 67!? Master them all and craft your winning strategy.

QUICK, ADDICTIVE FUN

Short, snappy rounds — perfect for pick-up-and-play anytime of day. Real math practice disguised as pure arcade excitement.

SEE YOUR PROGRESS SHINE

Stats screen breaks down accuracy by operation and difficulty. Kids track their gains, parents see the real wins.
